Refund & Cancellation Policy

We work hard to deliver high-quality digital products and services. Please review the policy below to understand how refunds and cancellations are handled for your purchases on OpenVault.

1. Digital Products

As our offerings are primarily digital in nature, access is granted immediately after a successful payment. Accordingly, once a digital product has been delivered, activated, or downloaded, we are unable to provide refunds, exchanges, or credits.

Please review the product details, demo information, and any available previews carefully before completing your purchase.

2. Service Cancellations

For services and subscription-based offerings, cancellation requests must be raised within 24 hours of purchase or activation. Requests submitted after this window will not be eligible for a refund.

In certain cases, OpenVault may allow rescheduling or adjustments instead of a full refund, at our discretion and subject to service type and usage.

3. Payment Disputes & Support

If you believe a payment has been made in error, or you notice an unexpected charge, please contact our billing team as soon as possible so that we can review the transaction details.

You can reach us at billing@openvault.in with your registered email address, transaction ID, and a short description of the issue.

4. Razorpay Transactions

All online payments on OpenVault are processed securely through Razorpay or other approved payment gateways. If a refund is approved under this policy or as an exception, it will be initiated only via the original payment method used for the transaction, subject to the gateway’s timelines and processes.

OpenVault is not responsible for delays caused by banking partners or payment gateways, but we will provide reasonable support and status updates where possible.

5. Policy Updates

OpenVault may update this Refund & Cancellation Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, payment partners, or regulatory requirements. Any updates will be effective from the date they are published on this page.
