Refund & Cancellation Policy

We strive to deliver high-quality digital products and services. Please review our refund and cancellation terms below.

1. Digital Products

Due to the nature of digital goods, we do not offer refunds once a product has been delivered or downloaded.

2. Service Cancellations

For services, cancellations must be requested within 24 hours of purchase. After this window, no refunds will be issued.

3. Payment Disputes

If you believe a payment was made in error, contact us immediately at billing@openvault.in.

4. Razorpay Transactions

All payments are processed securely via Razorpay. Refunds, if applicable, will be issued through the same gateway.
