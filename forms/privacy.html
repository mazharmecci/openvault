Privacy Policy

OpenVault is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with transparency and care. Please review this policy to understand how your data is collected, used, and safeguarded when you interact with our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with OpenVault, we may collect personal details such as your name, email address, contact number, and any information you voluntarily provide through forms, inquiries, or account registrations.

We may also collect technical and usage data, including IP address, device information, browser type, and pages visited, to help improve the performance and security of our platform.

2. How We Use Your Data

The information we collect is used to provide and improve our services, process payments, respond to your queries, and personalize your experience on OpenVault.

With your consent where required, we may also use your contact details to share important updates, transactional notifications, and relevant offers related to our products and services.

3. Data Protection & Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures, including secure protocols and encryption where appropriate, to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel and service providers who need it to operate, develop, or improve our services, and they are bound by confidentiality obligations.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Limited sharing may occur with trusted service providers such as payment gateways, hosting providers, or analytics tools, solely for operating our services and only to the extent necessary.

In certain cases, we may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or a valid legal request from authorities.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance functionality, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors use our site. This helps us improve usability and performance over time.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some features of the site may not function as intended if cookies are turned off.

6. Your Rights & Choices

Subject to applicable laws, you may have the right to access, update, or request deletion of certain personal information we hold about you.

To exercise these rights or to update your preferences for communications, you can contact us using the details provided below.

7. Contact & Support

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please reach out to us at support@openvault.in.

8. Changes to This Policy

OpenVault may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or data practices. Any updates will be effective from the date they are posted on this page.
